# Nvidia-V100



## 技术规格:

+--------------+---------------------------------+
| 规格         | 参数                            |
+==============+=================================+
| CUDA 核心    | 5120                            |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
| Tensor Cores | 640                             |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
| 显存         | 16GB 或 32GB HBM2               |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
| 显存带宽     | 900GB/s                         |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
| FP32 性能    | 15.7 TFLOPS                     |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
| FP64 性能    | 7.8 TFLOPS                      |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
| FP16 性能    | 125 TFLOPS（使用 Tensor Cores） |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
| NVLink 带宽  | 300 GB/s（双向）                |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
| 功耗         | 300W                            |
+--------------+---------------------------------+
