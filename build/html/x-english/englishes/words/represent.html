词根: represent¶

represent¶

vt. 表现, 描绘
代表, 象征, 表示
作为…的代表

时 态: represented, representing, represents
名 词: representability
形容词: representable
名 词: representer

词组｜习惯用语:

three represents 三个代表
represent as 把…描绘成
represent for 代表；象征
represent to 向…指出
to represent 代表

听力原声例句:

1 represents 5 minutes, and 2 represents 10 minutes.
1代表5分钟，而2代表10分钟。
Amazon is represented at the Academy Awards.
亚马逊提名最佳影片奖。
On the map, blue represents water and brown represents land.
在地图上，蓝色代表海，棕色代表陆地。
One animal represents each year, so totally 12 animals represent 12 years.
每种动物代表一年，一共12种动物代表12年。
That represent our planet from various fields.
他们代表了世界的各个领域。
Now, this view represents a full build.
这个视图代表了整体构建。

representative¶

n. 代表
代理人
adj. 有代表性的, 典型的

副 词: representatively
名 词: representativeness

词组｜习惯用语:

representative of 表示；代表……的
representative office 代表机构；办事处
legal representative n. 法定代理人
sales representative 销售代表
authorized representative 受权代表
chief representative 首席代表，主要代表
resident representative 居民代表；驻地代表
representative sample 代表性样本
trade representative 贸易代表；商务代表
permanent representative 常驻代表；常驻大使
customer service representative 客户服务代表，顾客服务代理人
representative government 代议政府
representative democracy 代议民主
representative value 代表值
customer representative 客户代表
house of representative 众议院
authorised representative 授权代表
marketing representative 市场代表，销售代表；营销代表
corporate representative 法人代表
representative institution 代议制机构

形容词:

Of course, these are undergraduates in Canada -- not the world's most representative population.
当然了，这些人只是加拿大的大学生，他们并不能代表世界上其他地方的人。
I don't think so. Our representative tours around the country very often.
我不认为如此，我们的业务代表经常出差全国各地。
That's the basis for the Western model of representative government.
这就是由代议制组成政府的西方模式基础所在。
Never stop working to do so—whether it’s a boardroom that is not representative or a campus that’s not safe.
董事会或许不太完善，校园也可能不太安全，但永远不要放弃努力。

名词:

As the chosen representative of the Seven.
您是七神的代言人。
And while we're at it, how about elected representatives, too?
既然我们同处一个国家，为什么不选举代表（来替我们行使权利）呢？
No; you can be much more useful if you will remain here as my representative.
不好。你留在这里作我的代表是比较有用的。
The reporters also were closely monitored by North Korean representatives.
记者们还被朝鲜政府机构严密监控。
This is the idea of a representative.
这就是" 代表人" 的想法。
The House of Representatives has 435 members.
众议院有四百三十五名众议员。
Are there hidden communications with Putin's representatives?
他和普京的代表之间有没有秘密沟通？
I'm the class representative for a relay.
我是班级接力赛的代表。

representation¶

n. 表现, 表示, 代理
表现…的事物, 图画, 雕塑

词组｜习惯用语:

knowledge representation 知识表示；知识表达
symbolic representation 符号表示；符号表征；符号性表象
graphical representation 图示；图解表示法
data representation 数据表示法
visual representation 视觉再现；可视化表示形式
mental representation 心理表征，心智表征
binary representation 二进制表示法；二进位表示；二元表示

句子:

Your memoir should be a representation of you.
你的回忆录应该代表你。
Is this an accurate representation of what you do?
你觉得这足以准确描述你所做的工作吗？
There are these big representations of knowledge.
因为知识具有很多种表示。
They are sort of a graphic representation of terror.
它们有些像是恐怖的具象化代表。
And they've got representation in the cabinet now.
现在黎巴嫩内阁中也有真主党成员。
This is a pretty dramatic representation of war.
极具戏剧性的表现了战争的一个方面。
Today there is so much more representation on screen.
如今，我们的银幕已经越来越多样化了。
He engaged me through my representation.
他通过我的经纪人找到我。
