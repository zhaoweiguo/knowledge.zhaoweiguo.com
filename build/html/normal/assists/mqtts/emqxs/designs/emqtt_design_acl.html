认证和ACL¶

EMQ 消息服务器支持可扩展的认证与访问控制,相关模块:

emqttd_access_control,
emqttd_auth_mod
emqttd_acl_mod

emqttd_access_control 模块提供了注册认证扩展接口:

register_mod(auth | acl, atom(), list()) -> ok | {error, any()}. register_mod(auth | acl, atom(), list(), non_neg_integer()) -> ok | {error, any()}.

http://emqtt.com/docs/v2/design.html#auth-acl
